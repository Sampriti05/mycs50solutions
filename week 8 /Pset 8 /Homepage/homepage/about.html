Who are we

We are a group of students passionate about giving our fury friends an opportunity to have an amazing owner hence we have founded this modern dog shelter called Lovedog! While our main aim is to match dog with potential matches of dog owners we provide volunteering opportunities to take care of the dogs in our shelter.We also provide a walk in interaction programme for dog lovers to interact with our dogs.

Lovedog is collaboration with dog shelters across New York City, as we come together to form a strong and supportive network. By joining forces with these dedicated organizations, we aim to enhance the welfare of dogs in need and create a positive impact on their lives. Through this collaborative effort, we will share resources, knowledge, and expertise, ensuring that every dog in our care receives the utmost attention, care, and opportunities for finding loving forever homes. Together, we will strengthen our adoption processes, provide comprehensive veterinary care, and offer behavioral support to help these wonderful canines thrive in their new environments. By forming this network, we hope to inspire others to consider adoption and raise awareness about the countless benefits of welcoming a shelter dog into their lives. Our shared vision is to ensure that no dog is left behind and that each one receives the love and care they deserve. Through this collaboration, we believe we can make a lasting difference and create a brighter future for dogs across NYC.
